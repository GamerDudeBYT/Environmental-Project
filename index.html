What the tracker does

Eco tracker website where you get set tasks at your own pace and get encouraged to complete them by earning points for completing tasks. You get more points for harder tasks. Tasks can be rejected if you want and you can have any amount of tasks at any time. You can choose what level tasks you get set. It saves locally by using website cookies. The tasks have 3 levels of difficulty:

  • Easy: 100 Points
  • Medium: 200 Points
  • Hard: 300 Points
